Film production company based in Mexico City founded by Salomón Askenazi & Isaac Cherem. Currently developing a 4 movie investment project which jump started with the most successful crowdfunding project on Fondeadora. Fosforescente produces films for a wide audience with fresh and original content. Our mission is to change the narrative and perspective of international audiences on mexican cinema.

Title: Ocean Blues
Production Company: Yellow Films, Fosforescente
Year: 2011
Genre: Drama, Romance
Length: 1 h 33 min
Director: Salomón Askenazi
Producer: Isaac Ezban
Cast: Xavier Villanova, Isabel Piquer
Synopsis: The film follows the life of Isabel and Xavier, both in search of love, both with different goals. Their stories intertwine when they decide to work together. Xavier gets lost in his passion for theater and his desire to be with Isabel, creating a metaphoric world in the beach, where the truth of their love life is portrayed. The story evolves when Xavier asks Isabel to star in his first play, an intense monologue titled "Ocean Blues".
Distributor: Cineteca Nacional

Films in post-production

Title: Leona
Production Company: Fosforescente
Year: 2018
Genre: Drama
Director: Isaac Cherem
Writers: Isaac Cherem & Naian González Norvind
Producer: Salomón Askenazi
Cast: Naian González Norvind
Synopsis: Ariela, a traditional Jew, meets Ivan, a boy who is not Jewish with whom he begins a secret relationship despite her family beliefs. Along with him, Ariela is exposed to the world that was outside her community bubble. Ariela is discovered and threatened. Films in development

Title: El Rey de la Fiesta (King of the Party)
Production Company: Fosforescente
Year: 2019
Genre: Sci-fi, Drama
Director: Salomón Askenazi
Writers: Salomon Askenazi & Isaac Cherem
Producer: Isaac Cherem
Synopsis: Hector is a shy psychoanalyst. A patient of his invites him to a birthday party. He intends to go, but his pregnant wife has birth complications so he spends the night in the hospital. Title: La Suegra
Production Company: Fosforescente
Year: 2019
Genre: Horror
Director: Mauricio Chernovetzky
Producers: Isaac Cherem & Salomón Askenazi
Synopsis: Carlos and May are getting married, but due to Carlos’ strict upbringing they still haven't had sex. After meeting his controlling mother on the night before the wedding, May rebelliously slips into his fiancé’s quarters and seduces him. The next day, as she stands on the altar, May notices that her sweet Carlos is no longer the same. As the party unfolds, he begins to lose control of his impulses, unleashing an epidemic that removes other guests’ social and moral filters, and turns the joyful event into an apocalyptic nightmare.
