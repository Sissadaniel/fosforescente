Film production company based in Mexico City founded by Salomón Askenazi & Isaac Cherem. Currently developing a 4 movie investment project which jump started with the most successful crowdfunding project on Fondeadora. Fosforescente produces films for a wide audience with fresh and original content. Our mission is to change the narrative and perspective of international audiences on mexican cinema.

Production Company: Fosforescente
Year: 2019
Genre: Horror
Writers: Sandra Becerril, Isaac Cherem and Salomón Askenazi
Producers: Isaac Cherem & Salomón Askenazi
Synopsis: Diana and Emiliano, a young couple, are getting married during the weekend outside the city. Lucia, Diana's mother, a fanatical religious from a strange sect, has forbid them to have sex before marrying, out of fear of the dreaded punishment of God. One night before the big day, Diana and Emiliano can't contain the emotion and end up having passionate sex in the open. Emiliano's mother-in-law notices something unusual, she smells like they broke the rules. The wedding begins and strange things slowly begin to happen. Diana, Emiliano and all guests try to escape desperately from a greater force that has taken possession of the mother-in-law and threatens to take their lives.
